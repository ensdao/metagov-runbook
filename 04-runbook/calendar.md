# Meta-Governance Annual Calendar

The recurring operational cadence the Meta-Gov WG runs. Most items live on the [governance forum](https://discuss.ens.domains); for voting surfaces and dashboards see [../reference/resources.md](../reference/resources.md), and [../directory.md](../directory.md) for who runs each item.

> **Term-cycle shift:** Term 7 moved the steward cycle from calendar-year (Jan–Dec) to **mid-year (Jul–Jun)** ([nominations thread](https://discuss.ens.domains/t/meta-governance-working-group-steward-nominations-term-7-2026/22157)). Exact Term 7 dates + election mechanics → [../01-governance/elections.md](../01-governance/elections.md).

## Per-term (annual) cycle

| When | Activity | How-to / notes |
|---|---|---|
| Term start −1mo | **Steward nominations** | `[WG] Steward Nominations Term X`; self-nominate. Vote threshold + dates → [elections](../01-governance/elections.md). |
| Term start −days | **Steward election** | Ranked-choice vote on Snapshot → [elections](../01-governance/elections.md). |
| Within 5 days of term start | **Lead Steward appointed** | By the group's stewards → [stewards & roles](../01-governance/stewards-and-roles.md). |
| Before nominations | **Compensation standards set** | Meta-Gov sets steward/secretary pay for the term ([EP4.8](https://snapshot.box/#/s:ens.eth/proposal/0x26a5c8dec547837495707e70446d1e7cd874a91f75753c602998f6e70083a266)); procedure → [funding-request](funding-request.md). |

## Budget / funding windows

| When | Activity | How-to / notes |
|---|---|---|
| ~April and ~October | **WG funding request** | Per-term, sometimes two windows: `[Social] Funding Request: Meta-Governance WG Term X` → [funding-request](funding-request.md). |
| ~Quarterly | **Endowment permissions update** | `[EP] [Executable] Endowment permissions to karpatkey – Update #N` → [endowment-permissions](../03-treasury-and-endowment/endowment-permissions.md). |
| ~Quarterly | **Timelock runway top-up** | karpatkey tops up the Timelock's operating runway when below threshold → [treasury-automation](../03-treasury-and-endowment/treasury-automation.md). |
| Monthly | **Compensation run** | Single USDC `multiSend` batch to contributors → [monthly-compensation](monthly-compensation.md). |

## Standing & seasonal

| When | Activity | How-to / notes |
|---|---|---|
| Weekly | **MetaGov WG call** | Tuesdays 9am ET in 2026; one thread per year ([2026 thread](https://discuss.ens.domains/t/metagov-working-group-2026-meetings-tuesdays-at-9am-et/21799)). |
