# Term 0: Founding (pre-Term-1)

**Dates:** 2021-11-08 ($ENS token / DAO launch) – 2021-12-31. The first governance vote (EP0.1) opened on Snapshot 2021-11-24 and closed 2021-11-29; the four founding proposals EP0.1–EP0.4 all voted Nov–Dec 2021. [src](https://docs.ens.domains/dao/proposals/0.4/)
**Cycle:** other (founding period; precedes the regular term cycle).
**Working groups active:** None. [EP0.4](https://docs.ens.domains/dao/proposals/0.4/) *created* the four WGs (Meta-Governance, ENS Ecosystem, Community, Public Goods) effective 09:00 UTC 2022-01-01, with first steward elections 2022-01-10, all outside this term's window.

> Note: "Term 0" is a descriptive label for the founding period; ENS does not formally number it.

## Stewards

No stewards existed during Term 0. There were no working groups to staff. The first elections ran in January 2022 (Term 1). [src](https://docs.ens.domains/dao/proposals/0.4/)

## Funding

No per-WG funding proposals exist for Term 0; WGs did not yet exist. The first WG funding requests appear from 2022 onward. [src](https://docs.ens.domains/dao/proposals/0.4/)

## Compensation

No compensation-standard or comp proposal was adopted in Term 0, and **no steward/lead/secretary/scribe rates were set**. [EP0.4](https://discuss.ens.domains/t/ep4-social-proposal-creation-of-foundational-working-groups-and-working-group-rules/8156) established only the framework: elected stewards are "eligible to receive fair compensation for their work," while TNL-selected stewards receive none. No dollar amounts appear this term. Actual rates first arrive in 2022 funding proposals. The DAO Secretary and Lead Steward roles did not yet exist (introduced for the Second Term of 2022 via EP1.8/EP12).

## Multisig

No WG Safe existed in Term 0. The only DAO-wide wallet is the **Governor Timelock / DAO Wallet** (`wallet.ensdao.eth`). See [addresses.md](../02-contracts-and-multisigs/addresses.md). [EP0.1](https://docs.ens.domains/dao/proposals/0.1/) transferred the treasury and ENS contract ownership from the pre-DAO `multisig.ens.eth` to this timelock (root kept by the multisig).

## Activity

- **EP0.1** [Social] Transfer ENS Treasury and Contract Ownership: moved control of the treasury, Registrar Controller, pricing oracle, `.eth` Registrar and `.reverse` namespace from `multisig.ens.eth` to the DAO timelock. Voted 2021-11-24 → 11-29. [Snapshot](https://snapshot.box/#/s:ens.eth/proposal/0xfe73d1b06675d6bc1cc074f440c347274d13c55b513ea02ec950efe639adbbb0)
- **EP0.2** [Executable] Retrospective airdrop: authorized transfer of 213,049 ENS (per docs) for accounts that owned another account's primary ENS name; cutoff 2021-11-01. Voted 2021-12-01 → 12-06. [Snapshot](https://snapshot.box/#/s:ens.eth/proposal/0xcf77c74696cab1d939936ae8684c0007297bed641f60896ad186354f036d725f)
- **EP0.3** [Social] Amend airdrop: folded 6,246 ENS (49 transfers) accidentally returned to the token contract into the EP0.2 airdrop, raising the total to ~219,295 ENS. Voted 2021-12-07 → 12-11. [Snapshot](https://snapshot.box/#/s:ens.eth/proposal/0x9ab53c76cee40d58cb27b244dfa5f9f2763bd8b97b1b4be1dd0f0bf706818fb4)
- **EP0.4** [Social] Create foundational working groups and working group rules: established the four WGs and WG Rules; five stewards per WG; first term begins 2022-01-01; first elections 2022-01-10. Voted 2021-12-13 → 12-18. [Snapshot](https://snapshot.box/#/s:ens.eth/proposal/0x899ead1d9b9b98f63f6a60dc0939bef55dbe365e78c6a550f07be969a47f148b)
