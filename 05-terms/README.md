# Terms Archive

A per-term record of how the ENS DAO's working-group structure has been run, viewed through the **Meta-Governance Working Group**. Each term has its own file with full stewards, funding proposals, compensation, and multisig detail. This index gives the timeline at a glance, plus the structural evolution that moved the DAO from four working groups to a Meta-Gov-only model.

Terms are governed by the [Working Group Rules](https://docs.ens.domains/dao/wg/rules/); EP numbering follows the [leading-digit = term-number scheme](https://basics.ensdao.org/executable-proposals) (EP _N_._M_ = proposal _M_ of Term _N_).

## Structural evolution

The DAO was founded with **four** working groups (Meta-Governance, ENS Ecosystem, Community, and Public Goods), each run by **five** stewards across two 6-month terms per year, commencing 09:00 UTC Jan 1 2022 ([EP0.4](https://docs.ens.domains/dao/proposals/0.4/)). The Community WG was dissolved at the end of the First Term 2022, leaving **three** WGs ([dissolution temp check](https://discuss.ens.domains/t/dissolve-the-community-working-group/12970)). The [Working Group Rules (EP1.8/EP12)](https://docs.ens.domains/dao/proposals/1.8/) then reduced each WG to **three** elected stewards plus a DAO Secretary as the 4th multisig keyholder (3-of-4 signing), effective the Second Term of 2022. [EP4.8](https://docs.ens.domains/dao/proposals/4.8/) merged the two 6-month terms into a single **annual** calendar-year term, first applied to Term 5 / 2024. The late-2025 [ENS Retro extension](https://discuss.ens.domains/t/steward-term-end-date/21986) extended Term 6 stewards ~4 months and deferred elections, breaking the Jan-1 anchor. Finally, the [Path forward on Working Groups for Term 7](https://discuss.ens.domains/t/path-forward-on-working-groups-for-term-7/22107) ("Metagov only" won the Copeland vote) made **Meta-Governance the sole Working Group** for Term 7 (1 Jul 2026 → 30 Jun 2027), dissolving Public Goods and ENS Ecosystem, moving toward 2-of-3 signing, and making the Secretary optional.

> ⚠️ **Open question:** Whether terms after Term 7 revert to a Jan-1 anchor or stay July-anchored is unresolved in the Path-forward thread.
