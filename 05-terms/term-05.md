## Compensation

No standalone comp proposal was ratified for Term 5. The standing [ENS DAO Steward Compensation](https://discuss.ens.domains/t/ens-dao-steward-compensation/18063) recommendation (MetaGov, posted Nov 2, 2023) was carried into Term 5 and operationalized through the per-window funding requests. Rates **AS STATED**:

| Role | Rate |
|------|------|
| Steward (6 positions) | $3,000/mo |
| Lead Steward (3 positions) | $4,500/mo |
| Secretary (1 position) | $5,500/mo |
| Scribe (1 position) | $3,000/mo |
| Governance (per steward) | 5,000 ENS per 6 months |

Term-5 Meta-Gov stewards specifically received $3,000/mo base + $1,000/mo WG discretionary = **$4,000/mo** total. ([special election thread](https://discuss.ens.domains/t/term-5-meta-governance-working-group-special-election/19814))

> ⚠️ **Open question:** No Snapshot/Tally ratification of the compensation post itself was located.
