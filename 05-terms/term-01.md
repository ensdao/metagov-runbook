## Compensation

**No standalone comp-standard proposal this term.** Per [EP0.4](https://docs.ens.domains/dao/proposals/0.4/) rule 10, steward compensation was set inside each WG's Q1/Q2 2022 budget; TNL-appointed stewards were ineligible (EP0.4 rules 8.4 & 10.4).

- **Meta-Governance** ([EP1.3.1](https://docs.ens.domains/dao/proposals/1.3.1/), in $ENS): elected steward comp = 100 $ENS per 3 stewards / month × 6 = 1,800 $ENS; "lead coordinator" comp = 150 $ENS / month × 6 = 900 $ENS; total 2,700 $ENS.
- **Ecosystem & Community** (in USD): Base $1,000/month per steward × 5.5 months × 3 = $16,500; Supplemental $2,000/month × 5.5 = $11,000; total $27,500 per group.
- **Public Goods** ([EP1.3.4](https://docs.ens.domains/dao/proposals/1.3.4/)): $12,000 total for steward compensation.

Rates as stated; not recomputed. Term 1 used only the "lead coordinator" concept (EP1.3.1), not a formal Lead Steward.
