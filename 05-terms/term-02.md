# Term 2 — Second Term 2022

**Dates:** 2022-07-01 09:00 UTC → 2022-12-31 ("Q3/Q4 2022") · **Cycle:** 6-month
**WGs active:** Meta-Governance, ENS Ecosystem, Public Goods

Transition term. The Community WG was dissolved at the end of the First Term 2022 by [EP15](https://discuss.ens.domains/t/ep15-social-dissolve-community-working-group/12982), so Term 2 ran with three WGs. This was also the first term governed by the [EP12 / EP1.8 Working Group Rules](https://docs.ens.domains/dao/proposals/1.8/), which introduced the Lead Steward and DAO Secretary roles.

## Compensation

No standalone compensation-standard proposal this term. Comp authority is the [EP12 / EP1.8 Working Group Rules](https://docs.ens.domains/dao/proposals/1.8/) (Rules 11.1 stewards/lead, 9.4 secretary), which state stewards and secretary are "eligible to receive fair compensation" but set no fixed amount. Per-WG comp was budgeted inside each Term 2 funding proposal (e.g. Meta-Gov "Compensation: $190k USD").

> ⚠️ **Open question:** Per-role monthly steward / lead / secretary rates for Q3/Q4 2022 are presented only inside budget-table images on the proposal pages and not extractable as verified text. Do not assume the Q1/Q2 2022 Meta-Gov rate (100 ENS/steward/month) carried into Term 2.
