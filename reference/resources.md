## Voting & proposal platforms

Multiple front-ends index the **same** on-chain Governor. They are views over one contract, not separate systems.

| Platform | Link | Use |
|---|---|---|
| Snapshot (`s:ens.eth`) | https://snapshot.box/#/s:ens.eth | Off-chain social & constitutional votes, Copeland rankings |
| Tally / Cactus | https://www.tally.xyz/gov/ens | On-chain Governor: proposals, queue/execute, params |
| vote.ensdao.org | https://vote.ensdao.org | Official wrapper routing on-chain + Snapshot votes |
| delegate.ens.domains | https://delegate.ens.domains | Delegation UI & delegate ranking |
| Agora | https://agora.ensdao.org | Alternate on-chain executable front-end |
